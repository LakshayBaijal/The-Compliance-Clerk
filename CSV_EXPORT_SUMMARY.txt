USAGE:

Process PDFs (generates both formats automatically):
  python -m src.main "Files"
  
This will create:
  - output/compliance_results_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS.xlsx
  - output/compliance_results_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS.csv
  - output/detailed_compliance_results_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS.xlsx (legacy)

Verify formats:
  python verify_csv_format.py

================================================================================
FILE FORMATS:

XLSX Format:
  - Sheet: "Extracted Data"
  - NA Permission Columns: Sr.no., Village, Survey No., Area in NA Order, Dated,
                          NA Order No., Lease Deed Doc. No., Lease Area, 
                          Lease Start, Lease End, Status
  - eChallan Columns: Sr.no., Vehicle Reg., Challan No., Violation, Amount Due,
                     Date Issued, Due Date, Status, Payment Status

CSV Format:
  - Headers: Same as XLSX sheets
  - Delimiter: Comma (,)
  - Encoding: UTF-8
  - Data format: Exact match with XLSX

================================================================================
EXAMPLE OUTPUT:

XLSX (sample rows):
  Sr.no. | Village | Survey No. | Area in NA Order | ... | Status
  1      | Rampura | 256        | 16534           | ... | SUCCESS
  2      | Rampura | 251p       | 5997            | ... | PARTIAL

CSV (same data):
  Sr.no.,Village,Survey No.,Area in NA Order,...,Status
  1,Rampura,256,16534,...,SUCCESS
  2,Rampura,251p,5997,...,PARTIAL
